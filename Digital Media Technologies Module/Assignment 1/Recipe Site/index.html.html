Akshi's Vanilla Cake Recipe

Ingredients

For the batter:

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp Vanilla
  • 1/2 cup melted butter

Instructions

  1. Whisk well the 4 eggs
  2. Add the powdered sugar, mix well
  3. Add the flour
  4. Add 1 tsp of baking powder
  5. Add 1/4 tsp of salt
  6. Mix well
  7. Sift before adding all the dry ingredients
  8. Mix without any lump
  9. Add the melted butter
  10. Mix and make a smooth batter
  11. Add 1/2 tsp of vanilla essence
  12. Mix the whole batter without any lumps
  13. Cake batter ready
  14. Grease the pan with butter
  15. Apply butter all over the pan
  16. Add 1 tsp of flour and swirl to coat on all the sides and remove the extra flour
  17. Pour the batter into the pan
  18. Gently tap to remove the air bubbles
  19. Pre-heat a utensil for 5 mins in medium flame
  20. Place a small stand
  21. Place the cake pan gently and cover it with lid
  22. Cook for 45-50mins on low flame
  23. Cake ready
